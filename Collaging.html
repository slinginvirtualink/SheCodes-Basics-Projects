Collaging is one of my absolute favourite artistic hobbies! It's a 2-D art technique of bringing different parts of various media together to make a new whole. Not to be confused with pastiche (pasting together), collage may sometimes include magazine and newspaper clippings, ribbons, paint, bit of handmade paper, portions of other artworks, texts, photographs, and/or other found objects & media. These pieces may be assembled together in a variety of ways including, but not limited to, being cut up paper glued on to a canvas, or digitally (which can sometimes result in a 3-D animated collage).
My favourite collages are from the Dadaists, some of which you will see featured here from the Centre Pompidou. The other collages are featured from Kolaj Magazine, and look pretty cool!
