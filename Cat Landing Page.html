I Love Cats!

💚 Especially ones with green eyes 💚

Green eyes are caused by a low number of melanocytes (or melanin cells), but the shade of green is determined by the activity of the cells.
Purebred cats are more likely to have more brilliantly colored eyes than mixed breed cats, although this is not always the case.
